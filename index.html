ABOUT

Pets enjoy spa days too!

Here at Poochie Bella's Pet Grooming Salon we are dedicated to making your pet look and feel wonderful. Our salon is conveniently located in Gretna, La. and is owned and operated by veterinarians. Our amazingly talented and compassionate groomers will pamper your pet from head to tail. Our groomers have a passion for grooming and produce quality work. Our motto is..... "Quality not Quantity." We are animal lovers and fully understand the human- animal bond. We will treat your fur baby like one of our own and deliver a relaxing spa experience. Our appointments are scheduled to decrease cage time and separation from their human:) Rest assured, we only use top quality products to get the finished look for your furry friend.
